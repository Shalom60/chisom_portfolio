Writing has been a part of me for as long as I can remember. As a teenager, it was my best hobby. However, 3 years ago (2018), I turned that hobby into a skill and began making money off it. Now, I get paid to do the thing I love (and am actually good at).

In three short years, I have written 200+ high-traffic content for blogs and social media - all SEO compliant.

I have a knack for research and quality control. This enables me to write authentically on a range of topics about varied industries including agriculture, public health, mobile tech, lifestyle, faith, education, finance, and more.

My writing and research skills, combined with my in-depth knowledge of SEO and digital marketing has helped my clients smash their goals with a success rate of 90%+. I believe the best content is not the one filled with airy words, nor is it the one where all i's are dotted. The best content is the one that finds its way to the right reader, at the right time, with the right value-adding information. I endeavor to achieve this for all my clients using core SEO strategies.

Clients continue to work with me not only because I help double their search rankings on major search engines, but also because they trust and rely on me to deliver credible content on time.

Over the years, I've seen the impact that well-written content has on society. So, I do not consider writing to be just a hobby or a paycheck - it is a veritable tool that bridges gaps, expresses ideas, and helps achieve goals.

This is why I have devoted myself to writing on topics across different sectors that are emotive and evoke change. Most of my voluntary works orbit around politics, technology, education, and religion. These are topics I hold strong opinions about.

All my personal content can be found here you can check them out to see if my personal style is a good brand fit for your next opinion showcase or lifestyle blog.

My driving principles are brand trust, brand awareness, and milestone achievements. If you are interested in writing services that would uphold your brand's core values, be sensitive to your pain points, and inspire customer action without compromising your standard, let's connect.
